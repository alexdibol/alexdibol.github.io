Research Pillars

Quantum-Inspired Finance

Developing classical algorithms inspired by quantum computing, topology, and geometry to build more robust models for portfolio optimization and risk management. My work on Hamiltonian mappings and topological risk landscapes pioneers a revolutionary approach to financial modeling.

Engineering Auditable AI

Specializing in AI architectures that prioritize transparency and governance. My work on structural reasoning frameworks and human-in-the-loop controls provides blueprints for secure and compliant AI integration into institutional workflows, ensuring accountability remains with the human team.

The Science of Engineered Intelligence

Investigating the theoretical basis of "emergence" in AI systems. By applying principles from geometry and topology, I propose methods to engineer new AI capabilities by design rather than treating them as accidental outcomes.
