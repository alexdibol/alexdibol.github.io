Research Pillars

Quantum-Inspired Finance

I develop classical algorithms inspired by quantum computing, topology, and geometry to build more robust models for portfolio optimization and risk management. My work on Hamiltonian mappings and topological risk landscapes pioneers a new approach to financial modeling.

Engineering Auditable AI

I specialize in designing AI architectures that prioritize transparency and governance. My work on structural reasoning frameworks and human-in-the-loop controls provides blueprints for secure and compliant AI integration into institutional workflows, ensuring accountability remains with the human team.

The Science of Engineered Intelligence

I investigate the theoretical basis of "emergence" in AI. By applying principles from geometry and topology, I propose methods to engineer new AI capabilities by design rather than treating them as accidental outcomes.
